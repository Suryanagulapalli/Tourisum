Favourite Places

Taj Mahal

If there was just one symbol to represent all of India, it would be the Taj Mahal.Construction of the mausoleum was essentially completed in 1643, but work continued on other phases of the project for another 10 years.

Golden Temple

Amritsar is world-famous for the beautiful and highly revered Golden Temple.It became a centre of the Singh Sabha Movement between 1883 and 1920s, and the Punjabi Suba movement between 1947 and 1966 in Amritsar is world-famous

Mysore Palace

The Mysore Palace is a historical palace and the royal residence at Mysore .The current structure was constructed between 1897 and 1912, after the Old Palace burnt down, the current structure is also known as the New Fort.

Varanasi Temple

Varanasi Temple is most famous Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. The temple is considered a central part of worship in the Shaiva culture by Hindu scriptures.Most recently by Aurangzeb who constructed the Gyanvapi Mosque on its site.
